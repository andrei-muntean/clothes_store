About ancamorar.com

Welcome to AncaMorar.com!

Creative brand developed by Anca Morar, a designer and fashion design student from Romania.
AncaMorar.com is the destination for girls that are looking for a sweet, bohemian or sexy looks,  for women who live free through fashion, art, music, and travel. It offers a range of different styles and size, including a custom sizes upon request. All reflecting the creativity and uniqueness of Anca Morar concepts, invoking attributes of femininity, spirit, and creativity in its design. Known for its playful femininity, the designs is a destination for party dresses, minis and maxis. More than clothing, Anca Morar aims to create a lifestyle that’s inclusive and sincere.

About Anca Morar

Born and raised in Arad, Romania, at 20 dropped collage and moved to Florida in order to help financially her family. Always with a different sense of style anda love for fashion in 2011 creates a facebook fashion page which reaches over 200 K followers. In 2014 along with a friend, Tara, decided to create a studio space and start creating. Anca started to make her firsts pieces while Tara designed fashion jewelry. Together with what they make they start going to arts and crafts festivals around Florida and selling their pieces. After making certain connections during festivals Anca was invited to participate to several fashion shows and expose her pieces.
She was part of the cast for a short film who won BEST LOCAL FILM at Miami Fashion Film Festival ¨Startup is the New Black¨.
Collaborations with fashion blogger April Golightly pushed Anca to try new approaches and styles.
In 2016, Anca decided to follow her heart and move to Buenos Aires, Argentina. While the move meant taking a step back for perusing a career in Fashion with time it meant moving forward. After studying a year to equivalate her diploma, in 2018 started attending the fashion design program, which is nr 45 best fashion programs in the world at Faculty of Architecture, Design and Urbanism of Buenos Aires University - also number 73 best Universities in the world.
At the present time, while studying she works on small projects with limited sale.
